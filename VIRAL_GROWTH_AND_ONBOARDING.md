# Viral Growth and Onboarding

**Category:** Growth Strategy
**Version:** 0.4
**Status:** Draft

---

## Core Principle

No marketing team. No marketing budget. MURMUR grows through architectural necessity, visual spectacle, social mechanics, anonymity intrigue, and the Shadow Gradient. The shadows are more interesting than the light, and the light is full of clues.

---

## Onboarding Flow

### Step 1 — Key Generation

Silent Ed25519 keypair generation. No PII collected.

### Step 2 — Network Mode Selection

"MURMUR can connect in different ways."

- **OPEN:** "Fastest. Your IP is visible to peers."
- **HYBRID:** "Fast, plus a Specter — your anonymous alter ego."
- **GUARDED:** "All connections through Tor. Your IP is hidden."
- **FORTRESS:** "Maximum anonymity. Tor/I2P only."

Default: OPEN. The explanation is brief and non-judgmental. No recommendation beyond "You can change this anytime."

### Step 3 — The Empty Map

Single glowing orb. "Invite someone to bring your map to life."

### Step 4 — Invitation

Unchanged from previous version.

### Step 5 — First Connection

Unchanged from previous version. Celebration animation plays.

### Step 6 — First Shadow Contact

This replaces the previous explicit Specter Prompt. Instead of prompting the user to create a Specter, the app lets the Shadow Gradient do the work.

When the user reaches 3+ connections, the Anonymous Layer begins to populate on their Pulse Map (they can see Specter nodes from the broader network via their peers' topology data). Within a few sessions, the user will encounter one or more of the following naturally:

- A Veiled Wave shimmer propagating through their visible network. Unreadable. Visually striking.
- A Specter Mark appearing on one of their connections (or on themselves, if a Specter in the network decides to mark them).
- A Phantom Gift appearing on a friend's node.
- An Abyssal Residue ripple crossing their Pulse Map.
- A Duel transcript or Whisper Chain artifact bridged to their clearnet view.

No prompt. No tutorial popup. The user discovers the anonymous layer through its effects. When curiosity drives them to Settings, they find the network mode selector and a brief explanation of the Specter feature. The decision to engage is entirely self-motivated.

### Step 7 — The Cascade

Unchanged from previous version. "Most users see something amazing happen around 5 connections."

## Organic Growth Loops

### Loops 1–8

Unchanged from v0.3 — architectural necessity, visual spectacle, status competition, topology as identity, bridge incentive, empty cluster problem, Echo Index discourse, physical world events.

### Loop 9: The Shimmer Effect

Veiled Waves are the primary ambient pull mechanic. Every session, Open-mode users see encrypted shimmers drifting across their Pulse Map. Each one is a reminder that there is content they cannot access. No nag, no lock icon, no "upgrade" button — just the shimmer. The shimmer is the marketing.

### Loop 10: The Gift Effect

Phantom Gifts appear on clearnet nodes with no explanation. Every gift is a conversation: "Where did you get that effect?" "A ghost gave it to me." Every gift is a recruitment event. The most beautiful cosmetics in the app come from Specters. Want them? Become a Specter. Want to give them? Earn Specter Resonance.

### Loop 11: The Mark Effect

Specter Marks on clearnet nodes are visible, mysterious, and unrespondable without a Specter. Every mark is a puzzle: "Why did a Specter mark you?" The recipient cannot answer without entering the anonymous layer. Marks create one-to-one pull rather than ambient pull: specific users are targeted by the anonymous layer's attention.

### Loop 12: Masked Event FOMO

Masked Events are visible to everyone, joinable only by Specters. The Summary Waves after events are compelling content that broadcasts what happened to everyone who was not there. Each summary is a story of something the user missed.

### Loop 13: Whisper Chain Artifacts

Completed Chains are readable collaborative anonymous creative works. Each is a shareable artifact that demonstrates what the anonymous layer produces. Each is an advertisement for participation.

### Loop 14: Duel Spectacle

Duel transcripts and live visuals are visible to all, votable by none except Specters. The audience experience creates desire to participate.

### Loop 15: ZK Flex

Verified anonymous authority is visible to all. "This Specter proved 900+ Resonance." The concept is shareable, mind-bending, and creates desire for the mechanic.

### Loop 16: Sigil Beauty

Sigil Waves are the most beautiful visual objects in the app and can only be created by Guarded+ users. They propagate to clearnet and appear in screenshots. Every Sigil is a visual advertisement for higher anonymity tiers.

### Loop 17: Abyssal Depth

Abyssal Wave residue is designed to be the most visually arresting phenomenon on the Pulse Map. High engagement metrics and zero readable content. Users see that the most active, most amplified content in the network is coming from the deepest anonymity tier and they cannot read a word of it. The depth is the pull.

### Loop 18: Council Authority

Phantom Council Waves carry the weight of anonymous quorum-verified authority. When a Council speaks, it speaks as a verified collective. The concept of hidden, self-governing anonymous groups with real influence is inherently fascinating and drives conversation outside the app.

### Loop 19: Shroud Prestige

Shroud Node gifts carry unique cosmetics. Receiving a gift from a Shroud Node is rare and visually spectacular. Users who receive one become walking advertisements for the deepest tier of the network.

### Loop 20: Censorship Utility

Unchanged from previous version. Users in censored environments adopt Tor/I2P modes out of necessity and become power users of the anonymous layer, enriching it for everyone.

## The Pull Funnel

The loops form a funnel. Each user progresses at their own pace, pulled by their own curiosity and the visible effects of the layers above them.

**Open → Hybrid:** Pulled by Veiled Waves, Specter Marks, Phantom Gifts, Masked Event FOMO, and the desire to create a Specter. Typical trigger: "I want to read that Veiled Wave" or "I want to respond to the Specter that marked me."

**Hybrid → Guarded:** Pulled by ZK Claims, Duel participation, Whisper Chain initiation, Sigil creation, Masked Event hosting, Dead Drop indicators, and the desire for stronger anonymity. Typical trigger: "I want to prove my Resonance without revealing my identity" or "I want to initiate a Whisper Chain."

**Guarded → Fortress:** Pulled by Abyssal Wave full content, Phantom Council initiation, Shroud Node operation, and the desire for maximum anonymity and the most exclusive mechanics. Typical trigger: "I want to read the full Abyssal Wave" or "I want to start a Phantom Council."

Each transition is a single toggle in Settings. No paywall. No approval. No gating beyond the structural requirement of the corresponding anonymity infrastructure (Tor daemon, I2P router, etc.). The app helps the user set up the required infrastructure with a simple setup flow at each transition.

## Seeding Strategy

### Items 1–6

Unchanged from v0.3.

### Item 7

Early adopters are given Guarded-mode setup assistance to ensure the anonymous layer is populated from day one. An empty anonymous layer has no pull. The layer must be active and producing artifacts by the time Open-mode users arrive.

### Item 8

Developer Specters are active from launch, emitting Veiled Waves, placing Specter Marks, and gifting Phantom cosmetics to ensure first-day users encounter the Shadow Gradient immediately.

### Item 9

At least one Phantom Council is seeded by developers and early adopters before public launch, ensuring Council glows are visible on the Pulse Map from day one.
